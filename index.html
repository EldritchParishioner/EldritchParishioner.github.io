ABOUT ME

Starving artist from Bandung West Java. I make illustration and comic for a living. I'm a comic artist from Indonesia. My comics are Raruurien (www.raruurien.com). I'm also working as a freelance game concept artist. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Visual Communication Design, which gives me a high advantage on art and design projects. I enjoy creating art with dynamic and harmonic colors

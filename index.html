PROJECT

A current project that i already made until this point. A monthly update will most likely to be announced regularly in a sunny day scenario. "Raruurien" is a heartwarming slice of life webcomic about Rien, a widow witch with two sons, living in a small mountain village. To maintain a peaceful life without her husband, she has to assimilate with the villagers, learn their culture, become a role model for her sons and also keep a low profile by confining her powerful magyx in public. Raruurien was initially published as one of three comics in Kroma Comic Anthology in 2016. The book won the Pakoban Award (2016) as the Best Compilation and TGR Award (2016) as the Best Local Comic. Raruurien also independently won Bronze in 10th Japan's International Manga Awards (2017).
